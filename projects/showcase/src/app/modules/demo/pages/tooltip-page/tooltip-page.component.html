Tooltip
Tooltip gives us hint or message once we hover over it along with the properties to set placement, tooltipcontent and delay dynamically.

An example of Tooltip

tooltip on left
tooltip on bottom
tooltip on top
tooltip on right
        <div class="tooltip-example">
          <div libTooltipDemo tooltipTitle='tooltip on left' placement="left" delay="200">tooltip on left</div>
          <div libTooltipDemo tooltipTitle='tooltip on bottom' placement="bottom" delay="200">tooltip on bottom</div>
          <div libTooltipDemo tooltipTitle='tooltip on top' placement="top" delay="200">tooltip on top</div>
          <div libTooltipDemo tooltipTitle='tooltip on right' placement="right" delay="200">tooltip on right</div>
        </div>

Assign the content of tooltip to tooltipTitle.

Assign the position (left / bottom / top / right) of tooltip toplacement.

Assign delay for the occurance of the tooltip after the hover as giben in the example.
